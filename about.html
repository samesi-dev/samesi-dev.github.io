/ 01Story
A story of hardwork and perseverance.

Hi! I'm Saman Mumtaz. I am a Website Developer who is based in Pakistan. I have been programming for over 2 years learning C, C++, C#, Python, Web Development (HTML, CSS, and Javascript), and PHP!

I am a Computer Science graduate from COMSATS University Islamabad. I got into web development when I was introduced to web programming course during my student years. It fascinated me and it hooked me on, so I decided to pursue this career. I started designing and most of my design skills and knowledge are self-taught. It was not a great period of design since every website I created looked awful. But I kept on practising then I went for learning backend development to put basic functionalities in my websites. I learnt PHP, SQL and I took it to another level by learning Node JS, MongoDb and React JS.

My goal is to become one of the best Full-Stack Developers, everyday I'm learning something new and practicing my skills doing what I love. I have prepared myself to accept the challenges into this ever changing world and I will try my very best to achieve my dreams and goals.

/ 02The Process
How i work.

When I start working on a project, I try to get all the information and data from my Client that are relevant to project such as goals, demographics and preferred aesthetics. After this, I start doing the research about the industry, competition trends and Other factors that are necessary for the development of the project. When I have all the data that I need my next step is to work on the wireframe and prototype using Figma tool. After the prototype is finished and the wireframes are finalized and approved, this is where I start developing the frontend of the website. And finally I code the backend with best practices in mind.
